Angela's Cinnamon Roll Recipe

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ cup butter, melted

For the filling:

  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Warm the milk slightly, then mix in the yeast and sugar. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until frothy.
  2. In a large bowl, beat the eggs and add the melted butter.
  3. Combine the milk mixture with the egg mixture.
  4. Gradually add the flour and salt, mixing until a dough forms.
  5. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 10 minutes until smooth.
  6. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm place for about 2 hours or until doubled in size.
  7. Roll out the dough into a large rectangle on a floured surface.
  8. For the filling, mix melted butter, sugar, and cinnamon in a bowl.
  9. Spread the filling evenly over the rolled-out dough.
  10. Roll the dough tightly into a log and cut it into 3cm sections.
  11. Place the rolls flat in a greased baking tray.
  12. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C) and bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown.
